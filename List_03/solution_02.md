## Task 2 — Hypergeometric Model

### Random Experiment
We draw 4 components from 20 without replacement.

---

### Random Variable

$$
X = \text{number of defective components}
$$

---

### Possible Values

$$
X \in \{0,1,2,3,4\}
$$

---

### Probability Distribution

$$
P(X = k) = \frac{\binom{5}{k}\binom{15}{4-k}}{\binom{20}{4}}
$$

---

### Success Definition
Success = selecting a **defective component**.
