## Task 4 — Poisson Model

### Random Experiment
We count error reports per hour.

---

### Sample Space

$$
\Omega = \{0,1,2,3,\dots\}
$$

---

### Distribution

$$
P(X = k) = \frac{\lambda^k e^{-\lambda}}{k!}
$$

---

### Parameter Interpretation

$$
\lambda = 3
$$

This means:
- On average, 3 reports per hour.
