## Task 1 — Binomial Model

### Random Experiment
We inspect 3 screws. Each screw can be:
- Good (G)
- Defective (D)

---

### Sample Space

$$
\Omega = \{GGG, GGD, GDG, DGG, GDD, DGD, DDG, DDD\}
$$

---

### Probabilities

Let $p$ = probability of defective, $q = 1 - p$

Examples:

$$
P(GGD) = q^2 p
$$

$$
P(DDD) = p^3
$$

Each outcome probability depends on number of D and G.

---

### Success Definition
Success = selecting a **defective screw**.
