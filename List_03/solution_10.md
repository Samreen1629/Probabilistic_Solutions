## Task 10 — Multinomial Model

Given:
- $n = 6$
- $p_1 = 0.4$, $p_2 = 0.35$, $p_3 = 0.25$
- $k_1 = 3$, $k_2 = 2$, $k_3 = 1$

---

### Probability

$$
P = \frac{6!}{3!2!1!} (0.4)^3 (0.35)^2 (0.25)^1
$$

$$
= \frac{720}{6 \cdot 2 \cdot 1} \cdot 0.064 \cdot 0.1225 \cdot 0.25
$$

$$
= 60 \cdot 0.00196 \approx 0.1176
$$
