# Task 3 — Permutations with Repeated Elements

## Formula

If objects repeat:

$$
\frac{n!}{n_1!n_2!\dots n_r!}
$$

---

## 1. MISSISSIPPI

Letter counts:

- M = 1
- I = 4
- S = 4
- P = 2

Total letters:

11

Formula:

$$
\frac{11!}{4!4!2!}
$$

Denominator:

$$
4!4!2! = 24 \cdot 24 \cdot 2 = 1152
$$

Result:

$$
34650
$$
