# Task 11 — Modeling Outcomes

## Useful Idea

The number of outcomes depends on **how the experiment is recorded**.

Key factors:

- whether objects are distinguishable
- whether order is recorded

---

# Solutions

## 1. Linear arrangements of colors

Balls:

- 4 red
- 4 blue
- 3 green

Total balls:

$$
11
$$

Since balls of the same color are indistinguishable we use

**permutations with repeated elements**

$$
\frac{11!}{4!4!3!}
$$

---

## 2. If balls are individually labeled

If each ball is distinct:

$$
11!
$$

---

## 3. Explanation

When balls are labeled, every permutation is different.

When balls of the same color are indistinguishable, swapping identical balls does not create a new arrangement.

Therefore the number of arrangements becomes smaller.
