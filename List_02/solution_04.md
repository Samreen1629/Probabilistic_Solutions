# Task 4 — Circular Permutations

## Formula

For circular permutations:

$$
(n-1)!
$$

---

## 1. Seven people around a table

$$
(7-1)! = 6! = 720
$$

---

## 2. Two people must sit together

Treat the pair as one block.

Now we have 6 units around the table.

$$
(6-1)! = 5!
$$

Inside the pair:

$$
2!
$$

Total:

$$
5! \cdot 2 = 240
$$

---

## 3. Two people opposite each other

Fix one person.

Opposite seat is determined.

Remaining 5 people:

$$
5! = 120
$$
